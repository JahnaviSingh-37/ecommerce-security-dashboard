## üé• Recording Tips

### Technical Setup
1. **Screen Resolution**: Record at 1920x1080 (Full HD)
2. **Browser**: Use Chrome or Firefox in Incognito mode (clean, no extensions)
3. **Zoom**: Set browser zoom to 100% or 110% for better visibility
4. **Close Tabs**: Only keep the dashboard tab open
5. **Clear Notifications**: Turn on Do Not Disturb mode

### Recording Tools
**Free Options:**
- **Loom** (loom.com) - Easy, free for up to 5 minutes
- **OBS Studio** - Free, professional-grade
- **QuickTime Player** (Mac) - Built-in screen recording
- **Xbox Game Bar** (Windows) - Built-in screen recording

**Paid Options:**
- **Camtasia** - Professional editing capabilities
- **ScreenFlow** (Mac) - Great for polished videos

### Audio Tips
- Use a good microphone (or at least a headset with mic)
- Record in a quiet room
- Speak clearly and at a moderate pace
- Smile while talking (it comes through in your voice!)
- Practice the script 2-3 times before recording

### Visual Tips
- **Cursor**: Use a cursor highlighter tool to make it more visible
- **Annotations**: Add text overlays in post-production if needed
- **Transitions**: Keep them simple (fade or cut)
- **Background**: Use a clean, professional desktop background
- **Colors**: The dashboard's gradient design already looks professional
